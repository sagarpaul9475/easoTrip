Sri Lankan architectural master piece Dunes Unawatuna hotel is an eco friendly boutique hotel near the beautiful Unawatuna beach(100 meters/ 5min walk away). Dunes has 10 garden-view ac rooms and a 2 bed room ac cottage in its peaceful and tranquil setting. Dunes offers free Wi-Fi in all rooms and public areas, air-conditioning/fan, hot water, tea/coffee ,rain showers and toiletries, water bottles, Safes, car parking,free beach towels and continental and Sri Lankan delights for breakfast. Railway station and bus station of Unawatuna is 200 meters away from the property and the Galle fort is 5km away. Airport transfers to and from hotel, round tours, free luggage storage, laundry, currency exchange, facilities are all available.
