Set in Manāli, 2.7 km from Hidimba Devi Temple, Lords Residency offers accommodation with a garden, free private parking and a restaurant. With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel offers room service and a tour desk. Manu Temple is 3.3 km from the hotel and Solang Valley is 13 km away. At the hotel, all rooms have a desk, a balcony with a river view, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, bed linen and towels. All guest rooms feature a wardrobe. Tibetan Monastery is 1.4 km from Lords Residency, while Circuit House is 1.6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Kullu–Manali Airport, 50 km from the accommodation.
