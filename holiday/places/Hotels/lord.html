Set in Shimla, 300 metres from Victory Tunnel, Hotel Lords Regency offers accommodation with a garden, free private parking and a terrace. The property is around 1.2 km from Circular Road, 4.6 km from Indian Institute of Advanced Study and 7 km from Jakhoo Gondola. The accommodation features room service and free WiFi.

All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. At Hotel Lords Regency the rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels

Jakhu Temple is 7 km from the accommodation, while The Ridge, Shimla is 1.3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Simla Airport, 20 km from Hotel Lords Regenc
