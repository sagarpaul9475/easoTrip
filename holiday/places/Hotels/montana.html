Set in Manāli, 5.1 km from Hidimba Devi Temple, Montana Blues Resort by Snow City offers accommodation with a garden, free private parking and a shared lounge. The property is located 3.8 km from Tibetan Monastery, 4 km from Circuit House and 5.7 km from Manu Temple. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi. The units at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. Every room is fitted with a kettle, while selected rooms will provide you with a balcony and others also have city views. At Montana Blues Resort by Snow City all rooms are fitted with bed linen and towels. At the accommodation you will find a restaurant serving American, French and Indian cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free and gluten-free options can also be requested. The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at this 4-star hotel. Solang Valley is 16 km from Montana Blues Resort by Snow City. The nearest airport is Kullu–Manali Airport, 52 km from the hotel. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.7 for a two-person trip.
