Well set in the centre of Manāli, FabHotel The Moon provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, free private parking and room service. This 3-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk. The property is non-smoking and is located 1.3 km from Hidimba Devi Temple. All units at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. At FabHotel The Moon every room comes with a private bathroom with a shower. The accommodation offers an à la carte or vegetarian breakfast. Circuit House is 700 metres from FabHotel The Moon, while Tibetan Monastery is 700 metres away. The nearest airport is Kullu–Manali Airport, 50 km from the hotel.
