Located in Leh, 1.4 km from Shanti Stupa, Pal Hotel provides accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. This 4-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. The accommodation features evening entertainment and luggage storage space. Breakfast is available daily, and includes buffet, continental and American options. Soma Gompa is 800 metres from the hotel, while Namgyal Tsemo Gompa is 2.5 km away. The nearest airport is Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport, 4 km from Pal Hotel.
