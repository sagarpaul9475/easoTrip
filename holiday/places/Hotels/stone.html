Situated in Nubra, Stone Hedge Hotel offers a garden. This 5-star hotel offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. There is a restaurant serving Chinese cuisine, and free private parking is available. At the hotel, the rooms have a desk, a terrace with a mountain view, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, bed linen and towels. All rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle. A buffet, continental or American breakfast is available each morning at the property. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at Stone Hedge Hotel. The nearest airport is Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport, 128 km from the accommodation.
