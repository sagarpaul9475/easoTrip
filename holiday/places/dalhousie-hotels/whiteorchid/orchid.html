Hotel White Orchid Dalhausie is located in Dalhousie. This 3-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. There is free private parking and the property provides paid airport shuttle service.

At Hotel White Orchid Dalhausie you will find a restaurant serving Indian cuisine. Vegetarian, kosher and vegan options can also be requested.

The nearest airport is Pathankot Airport, 80 km from the hotel.
