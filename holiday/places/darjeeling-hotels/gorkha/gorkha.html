Located in Darjeeling and with Tiger Hill reachable within 11 km, Gorkha Heritage Home provides a restaurant, non-smoking rooms, free WiFi throughout the property and a bar. The property is set 1.9 km from Himalayan Mountaineering Institute And Zoological Park, 1.7 km from Japanese Peace Pagoda and 3.1 km from Mahakal Mandir. The hotel features family rooms.

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a kettle. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at Gorkha Heritage Home also offer a mountain view. At the accommodation each room is equipped with bed linen and towels.

Guests at Gorkha Heritage Home can enjoy an Asian breakfast.

Happy Valley Tea Estate is 3.2 km from the hotel, while Ghoom Monastery is 7.6 km away. The nearest airport is Bagdogra International, 67 km from Gorkha Heritage Home, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
