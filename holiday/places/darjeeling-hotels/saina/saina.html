Boasting a terrace, Q SAINA MAPHAM is situated in Darjeeling in the West Bengal region, 11 km from Tiger Hill and 700 metres from Himalayan Mountaineering Institute And Zoological Park. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi. The property is non-smoking and is located 1.7 km from Japanese Peace Pagoda.

At the hotel, each room is equipped with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Q SAINA MAPHAM features some units with mountain views, and rooms are equipped with a kettle. At the accommodation the rooms are fitted with bed linen and towels.

An à la carte, continental or Italian breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

Mahakal Mandir is 1.4 km from Q SAINA MAPHAM, while Happy Valley Tea Estate is 2.6 km from the property. The nearest airport is Bagdogra International Airport, 67 km from the hotel.
