Tour Highlights

More Information

Our Darjeeling package offers a delightful journey to the "Queen of the Hills." Enjoy breathtaking views of the Himalayas, explore tea gardens, and immerse yourself in the rich culture of the region.

Trip Highlights

  • Witness the sunrise over Mount Kanchenjunga from Tiger Hill.
  • Ride the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Visit the lush tea gardens and savor the world-famous Darjeeling tea.
  • Explore the quaint town of Mirik and its serene Sumendu Lake.
  • Discover the vibrant culture and stunning landscapes of Kalimpong and Gangtok.

What to Bring

  • Warm Clothing: Darjeeling can be cold, especially early in the morning and late in the evening. Layered clothing is recommended.
  • Comfortable Footwear: With plenty of walking and exploring, sturdy shoes are essential.
  • Personal Medication and First Aid Kit: Carry any necessary medication along with basic first aid supplies.
  • Camera: The scenic beauty of Darjeeling offers countless photo opportunities.
  • Travel Documents: Ensure you have your ID, flight tickets, and hotel booking details.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Darjeeling is between March to May and October to December when the weather is pleasant, and the skies are clear for stunning views of the Himalayas.
